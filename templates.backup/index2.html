About This Project

This project was inspired by my passion for personal growth, community involvement, career advancement, competition, and the desire to make meaningful contributions to the world of software development. I do believe that getting the statistics for your work can tell how well you are doing. This serve as a source of motivation and fulfillment for many developers. This project also served as a portfolio project for Holberton School.
